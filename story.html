I was an average student in my initial time of school because I was constantly missing school due to many illness. I liked mathematics in my school days. Then I choose PCM in Class 11 th and started preparing for JEE. During my JEE days, I grew fond of Economics and Finance, thanks to Economics Explained and Finance Channels like Ankur Warikoo & Rachna Ranande. I am still exploring these topics and getting better by each passing days. I believe Finance has a role to play in everyone's life and Financial independence is necessary than anyting in today's world.

Let's come to JEE. I was only preparing for JEE Mains, I scored well in January JEE Mains.I was very happy that I would get in a Tier-1 NIT , but life had other plans. National Lockdown was announced in March, wasn't able to give my sixth and last subject board exam for which I was grateful. During lockdown, I studied like there is no tommorow and took my JEE Advance-2020.

I got a good rank and enrolled in Indian Institute of Technology, Ropar , doing many things which I would not have ever thought of, after coming to IIT and Life continues on.
