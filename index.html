Story of my Life

I mainly lived my life in Roorkee ,Uttarakhand. I was an average student in my initial days of school because I was constantly missing school due to many illness. I liked mathematics from my school days and still like now. Then I choose PCM in Class 11 th and started preparing for JEE. During my JEE days, I grew fond of Economics and Finance, thanks to youtube channels like Economics Explained and Finance Channels like Rachna Ranande. I am still exploring these topics and getting better by each passing day by learning new stuff each day. I believe Finance has a role to play in everyone's life.

Let's come to JEE. I was only preparing for JEE Mains, I scored well in January JEE Mains. I was very happy that I would get a seat in a Tier-1 NIT , but life had other plans. National Lockdown was announced in March, wasn't able to give my sixth and last subject board exam for which I was grateful. During lockdown, I studied a lot to improve my performance. Life was tough those days but I enjoyed a lot which I miss a lot now.

I got a good rank in JEE-Advance and enrolled in IIT Ropar. After coming here, I am doing many things which I would not have ever thought of doing in IIT and Life continues on.

Two Years have passed since I enter college and life is going on. Let's see what life holds for me.
