Story of my Life

I have lived entirety of my life in Roorkee ,Uttarakhand. During my school days, I was a shy kid who was ill half the time and I was not good at sports. I liked mathematics from my school days and still do. I choose PCM stream in Class 11 th and started preparing for JEE. During my JEE days, I grew fond of Economics and Finance, thanks to youtube channels like Economics Explained and Finance Channels like Rachna Ranande. I am still exploring these topics and getting better by each passing day via learning & checking new stuff each day. I believe Finance has a role to play in everyone's life and it is important to know about it in order to make better decisions in life.

Let's come back to JEE prepartion. I took local coaching only and did not wander to new cities for coaching. From starting, I was only preparing for JEE Mains, I scored well in January JEE Mains. I was very happy that I would get a seat in a Tier-1 NIT , but life had other plans. National Lockdown was announced in March, wasn't able to give my sixth and last subject board exam for which I was grateful. During lockdown, There was nothing else beside studying and this helped me to improve my performance. Life was tough during those days but those were the days which made me what I am today.

I got a good rank in JEE-Advance and enrolled in IIT Ropar.After coming here, I have studied various topic, which I would not have ever thought in my childhood. Three Years have passed since I enter college and life is moving on. In one year, I will moving to next stage of my life and hoping to get new expirience. Let's see what life holds for me.
